Meaningful Mahi

Photography

Web Design

Meaningful Mahi is a campaign to educate and encourage young people to consider disability support work. To help address the misunderstandings and stereotypes surrounding support work, Meaningful Mahi showcases three real-life pairings of a disabled person and their support worker. These showcases consisted of studio shot photography of these ‘matches’ alongside a series of objects that spoke to the things they loved to do together. As part of the campaign a website was designed to answer key questions and features video experiences of the pairings.
