Rewired is a non-judgemental programme for men who have sex with men that use methamphetamine and want to change their relationship with it. A new brand was needed for ReWired that could house information about the programme and details about upcoming events.
The rebrand consisted of a new brand system that allowed for easy use, and clear communication of the programme. Using intense hyper colour imagery to support the content, an image library was created that featured men in a number of different erotic scenarios. Working with an intimacy coordinater to direct and shoot these scenes, enabled us to be able to capture imagery that spoke to the intensity of the subject of ReWired, in a safe and controlled manner.

As part of the brand, a website was created that could communicate information about the programme, have a place where individuals could sign up for the programme, and provide resources for anyone that wanted further information.
